Once upon a time, in a faraway kingdom, a magical flower granted healing powers. 
The queen fell ill, and the flower saved her. 
Rapunzel, the princess, was born with golden hair containing the flowers magic. 
Mother Gothel kidnapped Rapunzel to use the magic for herself.
Rapunzel spent years in a tower, unaware of her true identity.
One day, a thief named Flynn Rider stumbled upon the tower.
Together, they embarked on an adventure to discover the world.
Rapunzel finally learned the truth and reclaimed her place as the lost princess.
